A littel about me

Welcome to my "About Me" page! My name is Roland, and I am a programmer and game developer with a passion for creating interactive experiences.


In my free time, you can find me coding away on my latest project or tinkering with game engines to bring my ideas to life. I have always been fascinated by the potential of technology to tell stories and engage audiences, and I am constantly learning and exploring new ways to push the boundaries of what is possible.

In addition to my love of programming, I am also an avid artist and enjoy drawing and painting in my spare time. Over the past year, I have also started learning 3D modeling and have been having a blast creating my own 3D assets and characters.

I believe that combining my technical skills with my artistic interests allows me to bring a unique perspective to my work and allows me to create truly immersive and engaging experiences.

I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow and learn, and I am excited to see where my passions will take me in the future. Thank you for visiting my page sand I hope you enjoy learning more about me!
